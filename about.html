Our History

During its initial years of establishment, SaffCoZ gained recognition for providing fragrances that embodied the authentic spirit of nature. The brand's initial creations were primarily woodsy, with notes of cedarwood, and sandalwood mixed with herbal accords, and amber to evoke the bright fragrance of sun-warmed wood or the peacefulness of a forest after rain. Every bottle had an invitation to immerse oneself in the most pure form of nature, in addition to its aroma.

By 2015, both nature lovers and perfume connoisseurs had taken a keen interest in SaffCoZ. The business grew outside of its native market and became well-known worldwide for its handcrafted methods and environmental consciousness. Famous fragrances like "SANTAL 33" gained fame because of their understated floral and green undertones that matched their rich, woodsy backgrounds, which made them both classic and contemporary.
